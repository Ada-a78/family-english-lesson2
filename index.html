课文逐句复听

1
Guoguo: Hi, Lingling. Did you travel this summer vacation?
果果：嗨，玲玲。这个暑假你出去旅行了吗？
Hi, Lingling / Did you travel / this summer vacation
2
Lingling: No, I didn’t. I stayed in Beijing. I went to the National Library of China on foot and read books there.
玲玲：没有。我留在北京，步行去了中国国家图书馆，并在那里读了书。
No, I didn’t / stayed in Beijing / on foot / read books there
3
Guoguo: That’s good. Did you also stay in Beijing, Maomao?
果果：那很好。毛毛，你也留在北京了吗？
4
Maomao: Yes, I did. I worked as a volunteer at China Science and Technology Museum.
毛毛：是的。我在中国科学技术馆做志愿者。
5
Guoguo: Wonderful! When did you work there?
果果：太棒了！你什么时候在那里工作的？
6
Maomao: From August 1st to 10th.
毛毛：从8月1日到10日。
7
Lingling: Is the museum far from your home? How did you get there?
玲玲：博物馆离你家远吗？你是怎么去的？
8
Maomao: Not too far. I went there by bus. Where did you go, Guoguo?
毛毛：不太远。我乘公共汽车去的。果果，你去了哪里？
9
Guoguo: I went to Hubei to see my grandparents. I learned to take care of plants and animals.
果果：我去了湖北看望祖父母。我学会了照顾植物和动物。
10
Lingling: Hubei is far from here. How did you get there?
玲玲：湖北离这里很远。你是怎么去的？
11
Guoguo: I took a train.
果果：我乘了火车。
12
Maomao: Wow! All of us had a meaningful summer vacation.
毛毛：哇！我们所有人都度过了一个有意义的暑假。

Think and act · School TV Show

Let’s talk about our summer vacation on our School TV Show.

  • Please tell us your name first.
  • How was your summer vacation? Did you travel ... ?
  • Where did you go? How did you get there?
  • When did you go there? What did you do there?
  • What else do you want to share about your summer vacation?

完整示范： Welcome to our School TV Show! This week, we will talk about our summer vacation. Hello, everyone! My name is Lingling. I’m from Class 1, Grade 6. My summer vacation was meaningful. I didn’t travel. I stayed in Beijing. I went to the National Library of China on foot and read books there. I also learned many new things.

本课五词精讲

形容词

meaningful /ˈmiːnɪŋfəl/

有意义的。搭配：a meaningful summer vacation。

All of us had a meaningful summer vacation.

介词短语

on foot /ɑːn fʊt/

步行；说 on foot，不说 by foot。

Lingling went to the library on foot.

专有名词

National Library of China

中国国家图书馆；前面通常加 the。

I read books at the National Library of China.

专有名词

China Science and Technology Museum

中国科学技术馆。

Maomao worked at China Science and Technology Museum.

形容词短语

far from /fɑːr frəm/

离……远；far from 后面接地点。

Hubei is far from Beijing.

语法与自然拼读

Did + 主语 + 动词原形 ...?

询问过去是否发生。回答：Yes, ... did. / No, ... didn’t.

Where / What / How + did ...?

分别追问过去的地点、事情和交通方式。

过去式

stay → stayed；work → worked；go → went；take → took；read 过去式拼写不变，读 /red/。

oo · /ʊ/

foot, book, good, look。例外：school /uː/，blood /ʌ/。

ar · /ɑr/

far, car, park, start。例外：warm 中 ar 读 /ɔr/。
